About Luxe Hair Salon

Established in 2015, Luxe Hair Salon has been Manhattan's premier destination for exceptional hair care. Our team of experienced stylists combines cutting-edge techniques with personalized service to help you achieve your perfect look.

Located in the heart of SoHo, our modern salon features state-of-the-art equipment and uses only the finest professional products. We believe that great hair is an art form, and every client deserves to feel confident and beautiful.

  • Licensed and experienced stylists
  • Premium professional products
  • Modern, comfortable salon environment
  • Personalized consultations
  • Flexible appointment scheduling
